Biryani

Ingredients: List of ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rise
  • 600 grams chicken
  • 1 small cup mint leaves
  • salt as required
  • 2 table spoons ghee
  • 3 green cardmom
  • 2 clove
  • 2 tablespoons kaaju
  • 2 onions
  • 4 green chilli
  • 2 table spoons ginger,garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon chicken masala
  • 1 table soon garam masala
  • 1 cup card
  • 1 cup coriander leaves
  • 4 cups of water

Instructions: Step-by-step instructions

Step:1

  1. meanwhile,heat oil in a deep bottomed pan
  2. Once the oil is hot,add cardmon,clove,kaaju in it and saute for 2min
  3. then u have tho add onions in it,and saute until broune
  4. now add chicken into it with slit green chillies,salt to taste,ginger garlic paste,red chilli poedwe.
  5. Mix well all the pieces and cook for it 2-3min,then add card into it and give a mix,(make sure the chicken is washed properly and patted drybefore adding it to the dish)

Step:2

  1. Turm the flame to medium again and add garam masalasin it corinder and mint leaves
  2. add water in it cook till the chicken is tender
  3. then add 2 cups of rice and spread evenly and add ghee in it
  4. you can now cook the dish without the lid or cover it a lid togive a dum,due th the steam formation

Step:4

  1. cook for 15-20min with aclosed lid and garnish with 1 tbsp fried onions and coriander leaves.
  2. Serve hot chicken biryani with raita...Enjoy!!
